Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need my own API keys? ▼
For the Starter plan, yes - you'll need to bring your own OpenRouter or Anthropic API keys (typically costs $1-10/month in API usage). Professional and Enterprise plans include API access with no additional keys required.
Can I change plans anytime? ▼
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences.
Is there a free trial for paid plans? ▼
Yes! Professional plan comes with a 14-day free trial. Enterprise plan offers a 30-day trial with full onboarding support. No credit card required to start.
What happens if I exceed my limits? ▼
On the Starter plan, you'll be notified when approaching limits and can upgrade anytime. Professional and Enterprise plans have no hard limits - we'll work with you to ensure smooth operations.
