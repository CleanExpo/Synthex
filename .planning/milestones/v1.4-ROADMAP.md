## Milestone Summary

**Key Accomplishments:**
- AI Chat Assistant with streaming responses and conversation history
- AI Image Generation with multiple providers (OpenAI, Stability)
- Revenue tracking across sponsorships, affiliates, ads, tips, merchandise
- Full Sponsor CRM with deals pipeline and deliverable tracking
- Affiliate link management with auto-insertion and click tracking
- Social listening with keyword monitoring and mention alerts
- Link in Bio pages with customizable themes and analytics

**New Prisma Models Added:**
- AIConversation, AIMessage
- TrackedKeyword, SocialMention
- LinkBioPage, LinkBioLink
- RevenueEntry
- ContentInvestment
- Sponsor, SponsorDeal, DealDeliverable
- AffiliateNetwork, AffiliateLink, AffiliateLinkClick

**Key Decisions:**
- Streaming SSE for AI chat (real-time feel)
- Decimal type for all currency fields (precision)
- Three-tier hierarchical models for CRM (Sponsor → Deal → Deliverable)
- Short code system for affiliate link cloaking

**Issues Resolved:**
- None blocking

**Technical Debt Incurred:**
- None significant
